Mis.matchmaker  V1.1

By Hagay Ladany


 
        Designing mismatch primers could be annoying and time consuming. To ease this task, this
        online app will predict the restriction sites for up to 18 single nucleotide polymorphisms
        around the requested nucleotide, as follows:   3' ... N N N(User request) N N N...5'

Enzyme list was taken from the New England Biolabs (NEB) website
